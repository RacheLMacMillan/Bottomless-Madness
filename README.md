# 🖋️Description🖋️
Bottomless Madness is a side view mix of realism, horror, survival, and zombies. Player need to survive in realistic conditions like making a mini reactor form mud, stick, and own shit.

# 🌍Lore🌎
## Lore of the world:
A cancer has a new stage that doesn’t kill a carrier but it incubate parasites that can control a body of the carrier.
## Lore of a player:
Player can choose some pre-lore variation about himself, such a fire-worker, a police officer, teacher or something else

# ✨Main Features✨
## Features:
- Mix of mechanics can make new mechanics
- Realistic zombie survival
- Side view camera like in Terraria
- Lots of visual effects 
- Procedural lighting of sprites (beautiful lighting)
- Advanced leveling system
